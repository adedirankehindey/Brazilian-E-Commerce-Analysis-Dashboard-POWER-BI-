## 🚀 Project Overview
This project analyzes the Brazilian OLIST e‑commerce dataset to understand customer purchasing behavior, seller distribution, payment patterns, and product demand across Brazil. The objective is to transform raw transactional data into actionable business insights using Business Intelligence tools.

The analysis produces an interactive dashboard highlighting order volume, revenue distribution, product performance, customer preferences, and seller activity across states and categories.

Key project highlights include:
- 99.44K total orders analyzed
- $2.91B total revenue generated
- 75 product categories evaluated
- 3,096 sellers included in the dataset

The dashboard allows stakeholders to quickly understand sales trends, payment behaviors, and product demand patterns across different regions.

---

## 🎯Problem Statement
E‑commerce companies operate with large volumes of transactional data but often struggle to translate this data into strategic decisions.

Key business questions addressed in this project include:

- Which Brazilian states generate the highest number of orders?
- Which product categories are most frequently purchased?
- Which payment methods dominate the platform?
- How does seller distribution affect marketplace supply?
- What customer behavior patterns can guide product strategy?

The project focuses on converting raw order‑level data into strategic insights that support marketplace optimization.

## 💼Executive Summary

The analysis of the Brazilian e‑commerce dataset reveals several marketplace patterns.

The platform processed nearly one hundred thousand orders across dozens of product categories and thousands of sellers. A small number of product categories dominate order volume, indicating concentrated customer demand.

Credit card payments represent the majority of transactions, suggesting customer trust in digital payment systems.

Regional demand is uneven across Brazilian states, indicating opportunities for targeted logistics expansion and regional marketing strategies.

Customer review scores remain relatively strong, suggesting consistent service quality across the marketplace.

## 💡Recommendations

1. Focus marketing campaigns on the top performing product categories.

2. Expand seller recruitment in high demand regions to improve product availability.

3. Optimize logistics operations in states with growing order frequency.

5. Use customer review analytics to continuously monitor marketplace satisfaction.
